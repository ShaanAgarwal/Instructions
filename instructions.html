Please Go Through The Instructions Carefully

1. Use the arrow keys to move the snake.

2. Avoid hitting the wall.

3. Do not press the key which is opposite to the direction of movement of the snake.

4. Avoid hitting yourself.

5. Intake of an apple will lead to increase in the point, and the size of the snake.

6. Intake of purple apple will lead to reversing the direction of the snake.
